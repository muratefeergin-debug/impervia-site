Impervia aviation image sources

Aircraft imagery is model-specific where practical. Clean manufacturer media is used for Cessna Citation and AW139; model-matched Wikimedia Commons imagery is used for Gulfstream, Bombardier, Dassault Falcon, and Airbus ACJ references. Website card crops are set so operator names and tail/registration numbers are not visible in the final layout.

Primary source families:
- Textron Aviation media assets for Cessna Citation CJ3, XLS+, and Sovereign.
- Leonardo Helicopters media asset for AW139.
- Wikimedia Commons model-specific aircraft references for Gulfstream, Bombardier, Dassault Falcon, and Airbus ACJ319.
