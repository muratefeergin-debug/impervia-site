About Impervia Group

Where capital meets
architectural ambition

Impervia Group is a multi-sector development and investment platform founded on a singular conviction: that the world's most extraordinary assets are built — not discovered. We identify prime opportunities across luxury real estate, branded hospitality, private aviation, and digital entertainment, then execute them with the precision of an institutional investor and the vision of a luxury curator.

We operate as both developer and operator. Where others hand off at completion, Impervia takes ownership of the long-term value equation — managing assets, generating income for residence owners, and building brands that endure across generations.

Founded by executives who have collectively managed multi-billion dollar portfolios, led landmark transactions across Eastern Europe, and built entertainment franchises reaching 100 million users, Impervia brings an uncommon depth of execution to every vertical we enter.

The Impervia Development Model

We take a fundamentally different approach to luxury real estate. Rather than developing and exiting, we stay — operating each asset as an integrated hospitality business that generates ongoing income for residence owners and builds brand equity over decades.

Each Impervia development is conceived around a central lifestyle concept: coastal, alpine, marina, or metropolitan. International luxury brands are brought in at the design phase to elevate commercial value. Clubs, restaurants, beach venues, and ski access are integrated into the fabric of the project — not added as afterthoughts.

Selected projects will include ski resort infrastructure, private beach clubs, signature culinary destinations, and members-only social venues — creating communities where the asset appreciates because the experience is irreplaceable.

1
You Purchase

Buyers acquire premium residences or suites within Impervia developments — at pre-launch pricing with full ownership rights and title deed.

2
You Own

Your residence generates a title deed, profit-share rights, and optional personal use allocation — with full transparency on asset valuation.

3
We Operate

Impervia professionally manages, brands, and rents your unit within the hospitality ecosystem — distributing rental income and maintaining asset quality.

Digital Entertainment

Joygame Publishing —
where play scales globally

Founded in 2009, Joygame Publishing holds the distinction of being Turkey's first gaming company — a pioneer in an industry that has since become one of the world's most valuable entertainment sectors. Today, backed by Korean gaming giant Netmarble, Joygame operates a global digital entertainment platform with over 100 million registered players across PC, mobile, and Steam.

As Impervia's digital entertainment division, Joygame represents the group's commitment to premium brand-building in high-growth sectors. The publishing arm maintains relationships with over 10 specialised game studios, operates the Joygame Select Steam label, and continues to expand through strategic title selection and world-class localisation. Joygame's esports ecosystem — the Istanbul Wildcats and Berlin Wildcats — extends the brand into competitive gaming at the highest level, having claimed the EMEA Masters championship.
