Who We Are

Where precision
meets prestige

Impervia Group is an integrated development and investment platform spanning ultra-premium real estate, private aviation, strategic defence, and global game publishing. We take projects from concept to completion, delivering assets that define a new standard of excellence.

Our approach is rooted in long-term partnerships — with landowners, luxury brands, institutional investors, and game studios — creating developments and franchises that generate lasting value.

Founded by seasoned executives with over three decades of combined experience managing multi-billion dollar portfolios across Eastern Europe and beyond, Impervia brings a rare combination of financial discipline, strategic vision, and luxury sensibility.

Experience-Driven Development

We create vibrant social and lifestyle hubs that resonate with ultra-high-net-worth individuals, elevating experiences through carefully planned environments.

Condo Hotel Model

Our innovative model combines luxury accommodations with ownership, ensuring residents enjoy five-star services while benefitting from prime location value appreciation.

Luxury Brand Integration

By integrating prestigious luxury brands into our developments, Impervia enhances exclusivity and design — attracting clients seeking unparalleled quality.

Global Digital Entertainment

Through Joygame Publishing — Turkey's first gaming company, backed by Netmarble Korea — Impervia extends its portfolio into digital entertainment reaching 100M+ players worldwide.
