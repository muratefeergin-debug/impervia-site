Who We Are

The Impervia Group is a multi-sector, international platform bringing together project development, luxury lifestyle, defence-related investment advisory, and digital entertainment under one strategic structure.

Founded by executives with deep experience across finance, hospitality, gaming, real estate, and regulated industries, Impervia focuses on creating niche, high-value platforms where intelligence, technology, capital, and international networks work together.

Across all its sectors, The Impervia Group's mission is to combine technology, investment, operational know-how, and trusted relationships to create distinctive platforms that elevate quality, efficiency, and standards of life.

Integrated Development

From land acquisition to asset management — Impervia controls every phase of the value chain, ensuring quality, timing, and commercial outcomes are never compromised.

Long-Term Value Creation

We build for permanence. Every project is designed to appreciate, operate, and generate returns well beyond its opening — for owners, partners, and residents alike.

Premium Execution

We partner only with world-class architects, international luxury brands, and best-in-class operators — because the Impervia name tolerates no compromise on quality.

Strategic Partnerships

Landowners, institutional investors, luxury brands, and strategic operating partners — we structure relationships that align interests and unlock opportunities unavailable to conventional platforms.

The Impervia Development Model

We take a fundamentally different approach to luxury real estate. Rather than developing and exiting, we stay — operating each asset as an integrated hospitality business that generates ongoing income for residence owners and builds brand equity over decades.

Each development is conceived around a central lifestyle concept. International luxury brands are brought in at the design phase. Clubs, restaurants, beach venues, and ski access are integrated into the fabric of the project — not added as afterthoughts.

Selected projects include ski resort infrastructure, private beach clubs, signature culinary destinations, and members-only social venues — creating communities where the asset appreciates because the experience is irreplaceable.

Co-Founder
Orlando Carlo Calumeno
Finance Executive · Investment & Development Strategist

Orlando Carlo Calumeno is a seasoned investment professional and finance executive with more than three decades of experience across investment banking, asset management, wealth management, privatization, gaming, hospitality, duty-free retail, and large-scale real estate development.

He began his career in the early 1990s as an investment banking analyst and later took part in international financial ventures across Kazakhstan, Russia, Ukraine, and Romania. Over the years, he has built a distinctive multi-sector executive profile, combining capital markets expertise with hands-on experience in regulated industries and complex cross-border investments.

Since 2007, Orlando has served under the Net Holding A.Ş. group as Group CFO and Executive Board Member, while also holding senior finance leadership responsibilities within Merit International Casinos Europe. In these roles, he has overseen group finance, corporate structuring, institutional growth, and major strategic transactions across Turkey and Eastern Europe.

His track record includes participation in landmark projects such as the Turkish National Lottery tender process, which reached a final bid level of approximately USD 3.2 billion. He also led Net Holding's duty-free business initiatives, including its partnership with Dufry, and played an important role in the group's international expansion across gaming, hospitality, and real estate.

Earlier in his career, Orlando was involved in pioneering financial transactions and investment products, including the launch of Turkey's first themed investment fund, tailored portfolio management structures, and the first international sale of Kazakh government Eurobonds. He also contributed to the establishment and development of local banking and financial operations in Russia, Ukraine, and Romania.

With a professional background spanning banking, capital markets, privatization, corporate finance, asset management, and real estate-backed hospitality development, Orlando brings a rare combination of financial discipline, institutional execution capability, and international project development experience.

Co-Founder
Doğan Murat Ergin
Business Development Executive · Finance, Hospitality & Luxury Brand Strategy

Doğan Murat Ergin brings more than three decades of senior-level experience across capital markets, asset management, business development, luxury hospitality, gaming, and real estate development. A graduate of Istanbul University, he began his professional career in 1991 at Global Securities and spent 25 years in the finance industry, holding upper-management roles at some of Turkey's leading financial institutions before joining Net Holding and Merit Group.

Throughout his finance career, Doğan held senior positions in investment banking, asset management, brokerage, client relations, marketing, and business development. His executive background includes leadership roles at Global Securities, Demir Invest, Ünlü & Co, and ING Asset Management, where he served as Senior Vice President of Client Relations and Marketing between 2003 and 2014.

During this period, he built one of Turkey's most distinguished institutional client networks, combining capital markets expertise with long-term relationship management and strategic investor communication.

After joining Net Holding and Merit Group, Doğan expanded his focus from finance into hospitality, gaming, luxury real estate, and international business development. As Chief Marketing Officer of Merit International Hotels & Casinos, he led marketing, brand positioning, and client engagement across the group's portfolio in North Cyprus, Bulgaria, and Montenegro.

He later served as Director of Business Development and Investor Relations at Net Holding A.Ş., contributing to international growth initiatives, strategic partnerships, and real estate-backed hospitality projects. He was also an Executive Board Member of fastPay Wildcats, extending his experience into digital entertainment, esports, and youth-focused brand ecosystems.

Doğan was one of the key contributors to Merit Group's Eastern European expansion and to the development of the Merit Starlit Budva condo-hotel model, a landmark ultra-luxury hospitality and real estate project with an estimated value of approximately €130 million. His professional profile combines institutional finance discipline, regional business development capability, luxury brand strategy, and direct experience in structuring hospitality-led real estate projects.
