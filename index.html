Who We Are

Where precision
meets prestige

Impervia Group is an integrated development and investment platform specializing in ultra-premium real estate, private aviation, and strategic defence sectors. We take projects from concept to completion, delivering assets that define a new standard of excellence.

Our approach is rooted in long-term partnerships — with landowners, luxury brands, and institutional investors — creating developments that generate lasting value. We target ultra-high-net-worth individuals and institutional partners who demand unparalleled quality.

Founded by seasoned executives with over three decades of combined experience managing multi-billion dollar portfolios across Eastern Europe and beyond, Impervia brings a rare combination of financial discipline, strategic vision, and luxury sensibility.

Experience-Driven Development

We create vibrant social and lifestyle hubs that resonate with the desires of ultra-high-net-worth individuals, elevating the residential experience through carefully planned environments.

Condo Hotel Model

Our innovative model combines luxury accommodations with ownership opportunities, ensuring residents enjoy five-star services while benefitting from prime location value appreciation.

Luxury Brand Integration

By integrating prestigious luxury brands into our developments, Impervia enhances exclusivity and design, attracting discerning clients seeking unparalleled quality.
